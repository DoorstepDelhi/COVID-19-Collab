About Us

We are maintaining a Centralised Database to manage the details of essential services i. e., availability of hospital beds, availability of plasma, details of hospitals, availability of oxygen cylinders, etc.

1. We work to provide APIs containing the verified data maintained by our as well as other websites.

2. We gather live data through scraping from verified Official Government websites.

3. We receive and relay availability of plasma from blood banks all over india.

4. We keep our data updated by accessing other websites, excel sheets and other resources.

5. We currently have 100+ active volunteers, continuously working to maintain the database.

6. More than 20 developers are improving this platform currently.

7. We are being supported by multiple NGOs and organizations from around India.

Volunteer Registration –

Volunteers would be required to register on our platform through the link : https://covid-19-delhi.herokuapp.com/rest-auth/registration
After Successful registration, the admins (i.e. we), will verify the volunteers entries and give them access according to the roles. The roles are mainly divided into following groups:

Viewers:

They would only be having the permissions to view the data and report it to get it updated or verified again. Contributors: The volunteers who cannot call and verify the leads (which might consists of 50-60% of volunteers) but can help gather them and add them to the centralized database will be added to the Contributors group. They will have permissions to add the data related to Facility Services (Oxygen, Medicines, etc.), Hospitals, Plasma and others.

Maintainers:

As the name suggests, they will be maintaining the database and also have the permissions to add new leads as well. Maintainers will be the ones who would be responsible for verifying the leads and keeping a check that no fake/fraud sources are circulated through our platform and would be able to report any of the leads through the platform to the moderators.

Moderators:

They would be having the permissions to add, edit and delete the data. Since editing and deleting needs to be handed to few trusted people, only verified and active people would be given access to this group. They will monitor all the reports by the moderators and the viewers. They will make sure the cleaning of the database regularly to remove unverified or inactive leads off the database so that precious time of the needy one won’t be wasted over wrong leads.

Admins:

Administrators have the access to whole database and will mainly be managing the user permissions and groups and the flow of the portal/ website. Any reporting regarding the downtime of the website would be solved on priority and regular updates/changes would be integrated without affecting the current progress/ database of the users.
The centralized database would be available in form of REST APIs (like the APIs provided by the Govt. of India on cowin website for vaccines). These APIs make sure collaboration from all the developers out there by not only utilizing our verified data, but also helping to maintain it regularly for their own platforms also (websites/ Application/ Bots).
